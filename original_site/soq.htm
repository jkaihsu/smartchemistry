Dr. J.P. Hsu (President of Smart Chemistry) is currently developing ASTM Methods D7650, D7606, D7652, D7649, D7651, D7634 and WK34574. These will be the standard test methods for sampling and analyses of trace contaminants in hydrogen fuel for FCV (Fuel Cell Vehicle).

Smart Chemistry Corporation has performed many hydrogen sampling at hydrogen fueling stations and analyses for fuel cell industries, including automobile manufacturers, hydrogen production firms and testing facilities at the detection limits lower than SAE J2719.
