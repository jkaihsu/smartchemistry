Trace Analysis of Hydrogen in Fuel Cell

To reduce the threat of automobile emissions, hydrogen fuel usage is on the rise due to its environmentally safe emissions - just water.  The caveat is that the hydrogen fuel cells themselves require an extremely pure source of  hydrogen. In other words the hydrogen used in these fuel cells needs to be clean of any other compounds or particulates. This poses a major obstacle to manufacturers of these high tech fuel cells:

  • Quality management. Manufacturers need to know the quality and reliability of their product - hydrogen fuel.

  • Progressive improvement upon current technology. Manufacturers need to know whether any changes to their processes actually impact the quality of their fuel cells.
