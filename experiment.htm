Verification

A series of experiments have been performed recently to compare the Health Cigarette Filter with regular cigarette filters using Marlboro and three popular cigarette brands in the Asian countries: Hong Jin Long, Huang He Low, and Hong Ta Shen. Third party experimentation was done by Wuhan Tobacco Company.  Experimental methods for this testing abides and complies with international methods adopted by tobacco companies around the world.

Conclusion

We conclude from the experiments done by Smart Chemistry Corporation, Wuhan Tobacco Company, et al. on the Health Cigarette Filter (HCF) that the HCF filter can indeed reduce significant amounts of carcinogen and toxic compounds in cigarette smoke without affecting nicotine level and cigarette taste.
